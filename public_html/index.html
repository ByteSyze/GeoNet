What is GeoNet?

GeoNet is a web app that creates a geographical representation of your Facebook friends based on their locations.

Essentially, this is the "I want to stalk my friends on a GLOBAL scale" app. How could that possibly be a bad idea!?

GeoNet developed by Tyler Hackett.
